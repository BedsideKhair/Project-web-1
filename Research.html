Copyright Creative Common (CC) Licenses, Fair Use

A copyright is an Act of the Law that gives the owner of a copyright the exclusive right to do and authorize others to do certain things in regard to a copyrighted work, including: make copies, distribute the work, display or perform the work publicly, and create derivative works.

There are four types of CC licenses:
1. BY - attribution required.
2. NC - no commercial use.
3. ND - no derivative works.
4. SA - Share Alike.

Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is a document that explains how an organization handles any customer, client or employee information gathered in its operations.

If I were to have a privacy policy for my website, it would be that all user's private information will not be shared within my website in order to respect their privacies.

5 ways to improve your website

There are 5 ways to improve your website listed down below.

1. Use images wisely. Don't spam them all over your website
2. Keep all of your webpages consistant to avoid confusion
3. Use the right color theme that matches your website
4. Get an idea of what is the purpose of your website
5. Think about which group of users will find your website useful

choosing the right web hosting provider

There are 3 factors when selecting the right hosting provider for your website

1. You should determined what kind of host provider you need for your website. Each will always has its advantages and disadvantages.

2. Make sure the host provider can also provide security for your website to avoid viruses and hackers.

3. Check to see that the host provider will be able to support your website.

Performance & Maintenance

5 ways to reduce page loading time
1. Evaluate your plugins
2. Enable HTTP keep-alive response headers
3. Compress your content
4. Combine images into CSS sprites
5. Cache your webpages

5 tasks for maintaining a website
1. Check all pages can load without errors
2. Make updates to website regularly
3. Check all forms are running properly
4. Remove any spam comments from pages and posts
5. Make sure your security is up to date regularly

Web Security

There is a chance that your website can suffer a cyber attack. There are 5 common ones and ways to prevent them.
1. Malware: Deadly viruses that disguises itself as a normal software. You need to use an antivirus and firewalls to prevent these attacks.
2. SQL Injection: Occurs when a hacker gains access to the database but can be prevented by using an intrustion detection system
3. Password Attack: Pretty much where a user's password is exposed to a hacker. This can be prevented by updating your password regularly and don't leave any password hints in the open.
4. Phishing Attack: Occurs when a hacker pretends to be a trusted contact and sends fake mails to it's victim. This can be prevented by making use of an anti-phishing toolbar.
5. Insider Threat: Another deadly attack that can cause massive damage to organizations. However this can also be prevented by having a good security awareness.
